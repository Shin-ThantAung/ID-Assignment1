Routine

Step 1: Oil Cleanser

During evening routin: start by using an oil cleanser on dry face. Take appropriate amount and gently massage dry face; rince throughly with lukewarm water.This help remove make up and built up dirt during the day.


Step 2: Foam Cleanser

Add a pea-sized amount of cleanser on your wet hand and create foam. Then massage on your face for 1 minute. Wash off afterwards.


Step 3: Toner

After drying your face, apply a proper amount using a cotton pad or your hand on your face evently. Pad your face a bit to help toner get absorbed.


Step 4: Treatment

There are many types of treatment. Serum is most commanly used in most of the routine. Serums contain many different ingredients based on their use. You should choose a suitable serum for your skin concern.
Apply a few drops of serum on your face.


Here are some ingredients to look for based on your concern.


Soothing & Calming: Green Tea, Tea Tree, Propolis

Barrier Repair: Ceramide, Snail Mucin, Niacinamide

Whitening & Brightening: Vitamin C, Niacinamide, Rice

Anti-Aging: Retinol, Ginseng, Peptide

Hydrating: Hyaluronic Acid, Ceramide, Propolis

Chemical Exfoliant: Hydroxy Acids




Step 5: Moisturizer

Apply a nickel-sized amount on your face evenly. Massage well. Moistrizer improve skin barrier repair. It also prevent and hydrate dry skin. Moisturizer creates a seal to make the serum more efficacious.



Step 6: Sun Screen

During morning routine, apply a decend amount of sun screen on your face and neck evenly. Sun screen is an important part of your skincare.
